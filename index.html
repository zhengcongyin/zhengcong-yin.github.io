Zhengcong Yin

Greetings. My name is Zhengcong Yin, graduated with Ph.D. from the Department of Geography, Texas A&M University. During my Ph.D. studies, I worked at Texas A&M Geoservices with Dr. Daniel W. Goldberg. My research primarily focuses on Geocoding/Reverse Geocoding Algorithms and Systems. I am also doing researches in GeoAI and deep learning approaches for remote sensing.

The overwhelming envision of my works is to allow people using location services for daily lives and academic research investigation seamlessly and to make novice users have more spatial awareness via geospatial techniques and applications. If you want to discuss about research topics or simply wanted to discuss how to apply location services to public health research/applications, please don't hesitate to send me emails.
